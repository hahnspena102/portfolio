Hahns Peña

Hello! My name is Hahns Peña, and I use he/him pronouns. I'm currently a fourth-year student at Cal Poly SLO, majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Computing for Interactive Arts.

I was born in the Philippines, and I moved to the San Fernando Valley when I was six. As an immigrant, I faced many challenges that required me to make connections and build a community in an environment I wasn't familiar with. Therefore, I strive to help create a sense of belonging both through my projects and my leadership roles.

About Me

For two years, I served on Cal Poly’s Pilipino Cultural Exchange’s board, where I took on the roles of Outreach Coordinator and Club Liaison. I organize large-scale cultural events with a focus on inclusion and collaboration. Furthermore, I coordinated with local organizations and campus clubs to strengthen outreach.

I also served as a Week of Welcome leader and worked together in a team of two. Guiding a group of 25 new students through orientation, providing information and mentorship, and leading conversations on key topics like bystander intervention, consent, diversity, and inclusion helped me become a better leader.

My passion is creation! Some of my hobbies include drawing, writing, singing, cooking, coding, and overall anything creative. I strive to create projects that uplift my community, showcase new ideas, entertain, and show myself off as a person. I started drawing when I was in middle school, and ever since then, I have been obsessed with the impact I could have from the things I created. I especially enjoy large projects that require teamwork, innovation, and perseverance. From video game development to songwriting, I hope that my projects provide engaging experiences to everyone!
