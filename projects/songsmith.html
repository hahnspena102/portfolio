Songsmith

Songsmith is a social media platform for music lovers to create, review, and share playlists.

GitHub

Key Features

Unlike traditional music platforms, Songsmith encourages community interaction through likes, dislikes, and threaded comments on playlists. It's built for music curators who want to express their taste and discover new sounds through shared experiences.

Create and share playlists in interactive threads!

Comment, like, and dislike playlists for collaborative music discovery!

Personalized user feeds driven by social engagement!
